About Us

Your Idol Finds is a leading Philippine-based online store that offers a wide variety of Korean Drama, Korean Pop merchandise, Korean Fashion, and Korean Cosmetics to quench the thirst of fellow Hallyu fans. Driven by the desire to help fellow fans to support their favorite actors, actresses, singers and bands, Your Idol Finds aims to be the trusted go-to online store for Filipino consumers for affordable korean needs, both official and fan-made, without compromising on customer service. All items are pre-order basis direct from South Korea. As frequent travelers and eclectic shoppers, we found ourselves in a constant search of fashion, beauty, and fangirling needs that represented our unique personalities. As a solution to this, we launched Your Idol Finds. Now, accessing products from South Korea is a breeze for not only us but the 100 countries we serve. We strive to enable discovery and instant access to korean fashion, beauty, and KPOP merchandises through constant curation and selection in hopes of providing you with products that enhance your everyday life.
